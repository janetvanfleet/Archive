Still from The March of the Teapots Video
Mixed Media, 2002

The March of the Teapots, a stop-action animation, follows about 70 mixed-media teapots from my studio in Barre, Vermont, along Route 2, to Catamount Arts in St. Johnsbury, Vermont. Here the teapots are spilling out of the front door of Studio Place Arts (SPA) in Barre, VT, at the beginning of their journey.
