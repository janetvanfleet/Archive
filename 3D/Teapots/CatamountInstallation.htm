March of the Teapots Installation
Video Installation , 2002

The March of the Teapots, a stop-action animation, follows about 70 mixed-media teapots from my studio in Barre, Vermont, along Route 2, to Catamount Arts in St. Johnsbury, Vermont. The video and the teapot actors were exhibited at Catamount Arts in St. Johnsbury in November, 2002, at the same time that the video was exhibited at Studio Place Arts (in an exhibit about food called "Clean Your Plate") in Barre.
