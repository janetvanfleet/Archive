Let Them Eat Bullets
Mixed Media, 2006

Although I don't give these cases titles when I exhibit them (so that people will be more free to create their own narratives), I have given them the titles that are in my own head in this website. This piece has lots of wonderful circular elements: a wooden figure with a round concave/convex belly, a small pot of axle grease, a ladle, and a group of shotgun shell casings.
