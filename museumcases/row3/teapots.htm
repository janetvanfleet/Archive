Teapots
Mixed Media, 2006

I've been making wooden teapots for several years. Some of the teapots in this case were featured in a stop-action animated video I made called The March of the Teapots (2002). Larger versions of the facing-off teapots appeared in an installation at the 2005 Art Hop in Burlington, VT that won second prize for outdoor sculpture (before it was vandalized...).
