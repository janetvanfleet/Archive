Museum Cases
Installed in the South Gallery of the Wood Gallery and Art Center, Montpelier, VT, June 1 - July 23, 2006

The configuration of the eight Museum Cases is flexible, so each space in which they are installed becomes a new “museum,” with visitors making new meaning out of the small worlds in each case. In this image we are looking through one of the cases toward the entrance to the gallery. Text can be seen in a continuous line around the gallery walls.
