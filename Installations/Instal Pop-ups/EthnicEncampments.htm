Ethnic Encampments
Red Mill Gallery, Vermont Studio Center, Spring, 1999

These pieces were created during "Vermont Week" at the Vermont Studio Center in 1999. The small figures (6-18" high) were constructed of wood, metal, clay, and fiber. Each "encampment" contained from from 3-8 figures with a similar morphology (their "ethnicity"), and they were displayed in separate groupings around the wall, or in long lines, as references to the refugees in the Balkans.
