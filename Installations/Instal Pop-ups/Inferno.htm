A Guided Tour of Dante's Inferno
Rose Street Artists Co-op, Burlington VT, 1998

This is a self-contained installation that is nine feet tall and 14 feet in diameter. The nine circles of hell are presented on nine descending surfaces. The suffering damned are constructed of unglazed white clay, and the "employees of hell" (the mythological and other characters who interact with the damned in the narrative) are all blue -- either fiber and paper mache or other clay figures.
