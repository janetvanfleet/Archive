Circular Statements
Installed at Studio Place Arts, Barre, VT, March 14 - April 15, 2006
Mixed Media, 8x6 feet, 2005-6

The original Circular Statements piece (8x12 feet), at right in this photograph, was built so that it could be flexibly installed at 6, 9, or 12 feet wide. This is a six foot wide version. In an attempt to work toward creating moving shadows behind the piece, I introduced an interactive element: high-intensity, hand-held flashlights with colored gels (one of which can be seen at the left of Circular Statements, in the middle of the photograph) were available so visitors could play light over the piece and create colored, moving shadows on the wall behind.
