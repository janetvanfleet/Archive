Targets
Installed at the Cooler Gallery, White River Junction, VT, September, 2006
Mixed Media, 32x50", 2006

This is a much smaller (and most recent) version of the Circular Statements work. It has the added development of layering the circular elements; some of the disks at the grid intersections have up to four layers, with painted tin disks of several sizes and a button at the center.
