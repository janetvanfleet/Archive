Circular Statements
Installed at FLYNNDOG, Burlikngton, VT, May-June, 2006
Mixed Media, 8x12 feet, 2005-6

Photograph shows Circular Statements at left, with Music of the Spheres covering the next two panels of the gallery. This exhibit was reviewed in the August-September, 2006 issue of Art New England. Ric Kasini-Kadour, the reviewer, said “The buttons stop being buttons and become like the stars in the sky. Their configuration could be accidental, a result of a million twists of fate, or they could have been placed by the hand of God.... This is where the brilliance of the work comes through."
