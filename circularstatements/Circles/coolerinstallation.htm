Cooler Gallery Installation
Installed in the cooler at the Cooler Gallery, White River Junction Vermont, September, 2006
Painted plexiglass and light, 10x14x8 feet

Four rows of plexiglass panels, ranging in size from 14x38 inches to 4x13 inches are suspended by monofilament from the ceiling of a former walk-in cooler. Four lights (two with gels and two without) on a timer/chaser project onto the panels, casting shadows on the wall from the opaque painted circles, and projecting colored circles on the wall through the transparent painted circular elements.
